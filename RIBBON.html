Pitch Ribbon

A free MIDI Pitch Bend controller designed for real-time expressive performance. Compatible with Yamaha and Korg PA keyboards.

Features

• Real-time ribbon pitch bend control
• Smooth and precise MIDI Pitch Bend values
• Adjustable bend range and sensitivity

• Compatible with Yamaha keyboards
• Compatible with Korg PA series
• 100% Free — No Ads
